About Us

PCC Global is a strategic investment firm with a legacy in infrastructure, healthcare, and project finance. Our Canadian diagnostic initiative builds on this foundation to address critical gaps in timely imaging services.

Our Solution

We are launching six diagnostic centers equipped with MRI, CT, and ultrasound capabilities. These centers will operate under a public-private model, reducing wait times and improving early detection rates.

Impact

Our centers will serve underserved populations, reduce hospital congestion, and improve clinical outcomes. We are committed to health equity, ESG alignment, and long-term sustainability.
